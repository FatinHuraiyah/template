Introduction

In the article, "Careers in Chemical Engineering" written by Jones, Wilkins O., he states that "the need of industry demand people with specialized knowledge of the vessels, equipment, and process necessary to produce chemicals on a large scale.". From his excerpt, we know that he is referring to the chemical engineers. In this endless-modernizing world, the role that the chemical engineers play is getting more significant, almost every industry involves chemical engineers and the demands for chemical engineers keep on increasing. Chemical engineers are needed because of the knowledge and skills they have in driving the momentum for technology advancement. So, what ability the chemical engineers have that allows them to assist in the world modernization? To understand this, we need first to understand Chemical Engineering as an academic discipline.

This report will give an introduction about Chemical Engineering as an academic discipline, illustrate the role and purpose of research in Chemical Engineering, characterize the communication that is used widely in Chemical Engineering, and describe the role technology plays in Chemical Engineering. Four sources will be used to help in explaining the issue discussed in this formal research report. There are two articles that are used in this Formal Research Report. One of it is "The Future of Chemical Engineering - an Educational Perspective.", written by Cobb, James T., Jr, Gary K. Patterson, and Ranil Wickramasinghe S. This is an example of scholarly article from Chemical Engineering discipline. It discusses in detail about the curriculum of Chemical Engineering Discipline. It tells the reader a lot about Chemical Engineering and how the discipline kept pace with the demand in the workforce. It also specifies certain skills that a chemical engineer must have in order to be recognized as a professional chemical engineer. Another source that will be discussed in this research paper is "Careers in Chemical Engineering.", written by Jones, Wilkins O. This is a popular article which also comes from the Chemical Engineering discipline and it focuses on the practical purposes of Chemical Engineering outside of the class surrounding and how it is apply to an actual situation which is the working world. It also discuss about careers that are related to Chemical Engineering discipline. The other two sources will be interviews. The first interview is conducted with Dr. Chong Mee Yoke, who is a professor has been teaching Chemical Engineering for 10 years and is still teaching General Chemistry in INTI International University. She has received Bachelor of Science and PhD in Chemical Engineering from University of Malaya. The second interview is an interview with Mr. Mohammad Nazroon Hardika who is a chemical engineer that is currently working with PETRONAS, the biggest Malaysian oil company. He has been working in the oil and gas department for PETRONAS for almost 40 years and he has Bachelors of Science in Chemical Engineering from Petronas Technology University. The purpose of these interviews is to have an in-depth in Chemical Engineering through the interviewees' life experiences regarding the issue that the report is trying to find out. The complete interviews can be found in the appendix section of this formal research report.

Apart from illustrating what Chemical Engineering is, this report will also describe the importance Chemical Engineering plays in society and a country's economy. After reading this Formal Research Project, the reader should have detailed understanding about Chemical Engineering as an academic discipline, tools and methods that chemical engineers use, the importance of Chemical Engineering in helping the modernization progress and contribution of Chemical Engineering to society economically.
