Role of Technology in Chemical Engineering

During her interview, Dr. Chong Mee Yoke explains the importance of technology, specifically computer, in Chemical Engineering:

"No matter if you are working in production, marketing, R&D or even management, computer will be your best friend." explains Mee Yoke. "The function you use on the computer might be different depending on the work that you're doing but you will still spend a lot of time on the computer." Mee Yoke adds.

Technology plays a vital role in Chemical Engineering. Chemical Engineering revolves around technology. The purpose of Chemical Engineering is to design processes for the purpose of production, transformation, transportation and proper usage of molecules, chemicals, materials and energy that is economically efficient. And in order to achieve these, chemical engineers must fully utilize the current technology. Chemical engineers are constantly trying to create new methods, tools, and machineries that will improve the production, transformation, and transportation and proper usage of substance and energy. This new discoveries, tools and machineries in evidently will contribute to technology advancement by increasing the economic and time efficient of a chemical process which in the end will help contribute greatly to humanity.

The excerpts from the article "Future of Chemical Engineering" illustrate one significant example of technologies that play important roles in Chemical Engineering which are the computer and internet:

"A new web-based capability, available in the labs at the Univ. Tennessee-Chattanooga (www.utc.edu), as well as other schools including the Univ. TexasAustin, Columbia Univ., Univ. of Toledo and MIT, enables real chemical engineering laboratory equipment to be controlled and monitored interactively by computers that are connected to the Internet. This would permit faculty and students from any university to run Web-connected experiments at any time of the day or night, any day of the week. The laboratory station computer operates the equipment (pumps, valves, heaters, relays, etc.), collects the data (pressure, temperature, position, speed, concentration, etc.) and sends it to the web user. The Univ. TennesseeChattanooga website is accessed via http://chem.engr.utc.edu/, and even includes audio and video of the operating equipment."

In order for chemical engineers to invent all those great inventions, they must also use help from technologies produced by other disciplines. Chemical engineers are greatly depending on other disciplines like electrical and computer engineering and mechanical engineering. For example, chemical engineers use computer, designed by electrical and computer engineers, extensively in order to conduct their research for data collections and operating their power plants among many other purposes. Turbines and other parts used in a power plant are invented by the mechanical engineers. Thus, chemical engineers are able to invent new technologies with the help from current technologies.
