Film Review | "The Social Network"

The Effect of Technology upon Writing and Communication:

"The Social Network", demonstrate a good example of effects that technology has on writing and communication. Nowadays, people tend to write more than in the older times especially among the youngsters. Lap tops, smart phones, tablets and computers has led to many other development in the social media like Blogs and Facebook as shown in the film. In the beginning of the movie, after Mark Zuckerberg got dumped by his girlfriend, he went to blog about his girlfriend to release his anger. This is very common in the present society and Zuckerberg is one out of many people that does this. People write blogs to share important events in their life, to express their opinion and thoughts on certain issue, to release their stress and to find new friends with similar interest and for many other unimaginable purposes. Technology allows people enjoy writing without them even realizing it. This is because writing on social media is very different than writing in classes because it is not gradable, you are free to write on anything you want, and it does not have too much formality in it.

Technology has led to many significant changes in how people communicate with each other. It allows people to communicate with each other regardless of the time and place they are at. This can be for the better or the worse, depending on how the individual utilize it. The film has demonstrated how technology can be used for both. Zuckerberg has insulted her girlfriend on his blog after he got dumped by her. He insulted her on blog which means the public can view it freely. This has hurt the image of his girlfriend gravely. Besides that, due to his uncontrollable anger, he has created website called "Facemash" where it allows the men to rank the women's beauty without the women's approvals. From this example, we can see how technology can be utilized to jeopardize someone's image and in some cases without that person's knowledge. This can hurt people's confidence and increase insecurities in others because people are allowed to say or do mean things to each other without facing serious consequences. On the bright side, from the film too we know that technology also improve the communication between people. Facebook, an online social networking website helps people to meet other people and learn something about them without actually intruding their privacy because the users only share information that they want others to know. This is a really great way of meeting new people, thus the Facebook grows in popularity both in the movie and also in reality.

In conclusion, from the film, we know that technology has both pros and cons on writing and communication. Since they are lots of freedom given in the technology used for writing and communication, people are allowed to do whatever they want. In order to have civilized and polite writing and communication among people, a proper education must be given to the publics since elementary school. This can help to ensure that the technology will not be misused by the publics for unwanted purposes.
