Executive Summary

Live on fast food and you'll die young. The purpose of this proposal is to find out the effects fast food chain has on United States' food industry and how does it affects the Americans. Food, Inc. is an American documentary film that discuss about the fast food industry and how it affects the Americans lives. It reveals the secrets that the food industries do not want us to know. Most people are unaware of where their food comes from and how they are made. Partly it is because they are ignorant but mostly it is because the food industry does not want them to know! Numerous disease results from the expanding growth of fast food industry. It is shown that the rise in fast food industry success is parallel to the rise in obesity level of public in the United States. Immediate steps must be taken to reverse the effects of this fast food culture. Other than trying to change the policy governing the food industry, small changes can simply be made by monitoring what goes into the kitchen's cabinets and refrigerator. If this situation is left amok, it will have adverse effects not only to the United States but also to the world. The number of people who is obese and suffer cardiovascular disease, type-2 diabetes or even cancer will increase constantly. The most worrying part is there will be more children that will be the victims of these diseases. Therefore, this is a very important issue and immediate steps must be taken to undo the effects before it is too late!
